CoHo Club History

Since 2023, Coho at St. Martin's Engineering College is the most active & a dynamic coding club nurturing innovation, collaboration, and excellence in software engineering.

At Coho, students take the lead in teaching fellow students both Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and web development. Thank you for visiting.
